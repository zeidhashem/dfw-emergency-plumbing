What DFW Emergency Does

DFW Emergency is a 24/7 emergency plumbing dispatch and referral service for the Dallas–Fort Worth metro area. We are not a plumbing contractor.

When a homeowner has a plumbing emergency, we answer the call, collect the basics, and connect them with a licensed, vetted plumbing company that handles urgent calls in their part of DFW. The plumber then contacts the homeowner directly with pricing and an estimated time of arrival.

Emergency Problems We Help With

If water or sewage is where it shouldn’t be, treat it as an emergency. Common issues include:

  • Burst or leaking pipes in walls, ceilings, or under sinks
  • Active flooding or standing water inside the home
  • Sewage backups and clogged main lines
  • Overflowing toilets that won’t clear
  • No hot water or leaking water heaters
  • Slab leaks or unexplained wet spots on floors
  • Sudden loss of water pressure throughout the home

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a plumbing company?

No. DFW Emergency is a dispatch and referral service. We are not a licensed plumbing contractor. All plumbing work is performed by independent licensed plumbing companies.

How do I pay for the work?

You pay the plumbing company directly after the job. Most accept cash, debit or credit cards, and some offer financing options.

Do you charge extra fees because you’re in the middle?

Any pricing for the job comes directly from the licensed plumber before work begins. Our role is to help you reach an available emergency plumber quickly.

Do you only handle true emergencies?

Our priority is urgent situations like flooding, sewage backups, and no water. In some cases, we may help route non-emergency jobs depending on availability.

Call our dispatch line and we’ll work to connect you with a licensed plumber as quickly as possible.
